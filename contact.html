Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse our course directory, select a course that interests you, and click 'Enroll Now'. You'll be guided through the registration process.

Are the courses certified?

Many of our courses offer certifications. Look for the "Certified" badge on course listings to identify which ones provide official certificates.

Can I get mentorship support?

Yes! We have a network of experienced Goan professionals who provide mentorship. Visit our Mentors page to connect with them.

Is financial assistance available?

We offer various scholarship and payment plan options. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and available support.
